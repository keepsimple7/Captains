About Us

“Nothing brings people together like good food.”

Captain’s Kook is a mobile-app and web-based platform that connects Mumbaikars hungry for good food with over 250 home cooks-turned-chefs who are passionate about bringing delicious food to your table.

Captain’s Kook aims to satiate the growing appetite for freshly cooked homemade food, whether it is breakfast, lunch, snacks or dinner, without the cumbersome ‘cooking’ routine.

Happiness is “Homemade”

For those of you missing your ‘Ghar ka khana’, or too busy to cook a healthy meal, or worried if fast food is taking over your fast-paced lifestyle, Captain’s Kook offers you happiness in a click.

Our highly talented chefs, handpicked based on our stringent guidelines, offer you a choice of tasty and healthy homemade food, specially curated by our F&B department to cater to the diverse palates of the cosmopolitan Mumbaikar.

Cooked only on order, prepared with the freshest of ingredients and seasoned with love by our home chefs, Captain’s Kook delivers food that remains fresh, tasty and nutritious, unlike restaurant food or food packed in lunch boxes for over 3 hours.

So order your meal and allow us to satisfy your craving for good food with our lip-smacking homemade delicacies delivered right to your doorstep!
